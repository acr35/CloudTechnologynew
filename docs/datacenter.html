What are Data Centers?

Internet requires lots of machines to serve all the different sites that you visit. However, the process of crawling the entire web, building up search indexes and having the machines available to quickly and accurately respond to search queries requires large numbers of machines. These machines are located in data centers. Data centers hosts thousands of machines known as servers. Cloud basically refers to storing and hosting your files in data center.
